---
name: Pizza tomato sauce
cuisine: Italian
meal: Condiment
effort: weeknight
tags: [base-recipe]
description: A no-cook Neapolitan-style sauce. Cooks on the pizza, doesn't need cooking first.

prepTime: PT5M
recipeYield: ~200ml (enough for 3–4 pizzas)

recipeIngredient:
- 200g passata
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp fine salt
- Pinch of sugar (optional — balances acidity if the passata is sharp)

recipeInstructions:
- Whisk everything together in a bowl.
- Taste and adjust salt; add a pinch of sugar if it tastes sharp.
---

Use a smooth Italian passata (e.g. Mutti) rather than chopped tomatoes.
