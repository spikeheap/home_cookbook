recipeInstructions:
- items:
  - Light the barbecue with a 50/50 charcoal setup and soak a handful of applewood
    chips per the packet. Strip the leaves from half the herbs into a pestle and
    mortar, pound with salt and pepper, then muddle in 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
    Cut down the chicken's back to butterfly it flat. Rub all over with the herby
    oil, then sit it skin-side down on the hot zone for 5 minutes, flipping halfway.
    Halve and char 2 lemons alongside. Put the bread on the cool zone.
  - Drain the wood chips and scatter on the hot zone with the remaining herb sprigs.
    Move the chicken onto the bread with its legs pointing toward the medium zone.
    Place the cabbage on the hot zone. Prick the chillies and sit them on the
    medium-cool zone. Cover with the lid, top vent half open, and cook for 1 hour
    until the chicken's internal temperature reaches 65–70°C, spritzing with oil
    occasionally. Lift off the lemons and chillies after 20 minutes.
  - Once cool enough to handle, scrape the blackened skin off the chillies, discard
    seeds and stalks, slice lengthways and lay on a plate with the anchovies.
    Squeeze over the juice of half a fresh lemon, sprinkle with the dried oregano,
    drizzle with extra virgin olive oil, and set aside.
  - For the dressing, bash the garlic into a paste in the pestle and mortar. Mix in
    the mustard, Worcestershire sauce and yoghurt. Finely grate in the Parmesan,
    squeeze in 3 of the charred lemon halves, then finely chop and add one of them
    along with a drizzle of the anchovy tin oil. Season to perfection.
  - Move the chicken and bread to a board and rest the chicken for 30 minutes (75°C target internal). Trim the broccoli, halving any
    thicker stalks lengthways. Lay the bacon on the hot zone, scatter the broccoli
    over the top and cook with the lid on, top vent half open, for 10 to 15 minutes,
    lifting off once golden.
  - Tear up the toasts. Click apart the lettuce leaves. Trim the cabbage, remove
    any burnt outer layers and roughly slice. Toss everything through the dressing
    with the bacon and broccoli. Serve with the golden chicken and the anchovies.
