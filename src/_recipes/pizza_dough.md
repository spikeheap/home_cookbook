recipeIngredient:
- items:
  - quantity: 250
    unit: g
    item: 00 flour (or strong bread flour in a pinch)
  - quantity: 160
    unit: ml
    item: water
  - quantity: 20
    unit: ml
    item: olive oil
  - quantity: 5
    unit: g
    item: fast-acting yeast
  - quantity: 5
    unit: g
    item: salt
recipeInstructions:
- items:
  - Add all the ingredients in a bowl (keeping salt and yeast as separate as possible).
  - Mix and knead for 5-10 minutes.
  - Leave the dough to rise "from morning to evening", or as long as possible. If
    you need it sooner, consider slightly more yeast!
  - Split into ~150g balls.
  - Prove these for a further 2 hours if possible.
  - Spread them out with fingers into 6 to 7 inch circles until they are the same
    thickness all over. Toss/spin the dough on your knuckles to make the dough to
    be thinner in the middle and thicker on the edges.
