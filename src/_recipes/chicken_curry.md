  - quantity: 1
    unit: handful

recipeInstructions:
- items:
  - Remove the chicken thigh skins (roast with salt for 20-25 minutes to crisp). Mix
    the yoghurt, salt, garam masala, turmeric, chilli powder and garlic. Add chicken
    and set aside.
  - Heat the oil in a pan and add the cumin seeds. Once they begin to sizzle, add
    the onions and cook for 10 to 12 minutes on a medium heat, or until they turn
    golden.
  - Add the tomatoes, cover and cook for 15 minutes on a low heat.
  - Season
  - Add the marinated chicken, mix well and bring to a boil. Cover and cook on a medium-low
    heat for 40 to 45 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  - Cook the rice.
  - Sprinkle coriander over to serve.
