Usage: {% include ingredient_item.html item=item inline_sub=true %} `inline_sub` is a tri-state: pass false explicitly to suppress the expansion (used when rendering an already-inlined sub-recipe to avoid recursion). Anything else (including unset/nil) means "expand".
